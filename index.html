Refinancia is a debt collector that has a business structure similar to that of a call center. One of the main challenges that face a company such as this is the turnover of its workforce. In fact, according to data provided by the company, each month, on average, 5.4% of the employees are dismissed or resign, which translates in a high challenge in terms of hiring and training for the human resources team

In this sense, the aim of the project is to develop and deliver a high-end analytics tool for estimating which agents are likely to either leave his job or be replaced on the workspace during the next three months since the date of analysis

We found that, when differentiating the employees as active and dismissed/resigned, there is a clear difference in their performance measured by the goal fulfillment of their expected monthly collection of past-due debts. Furthermore, we also found the tenure is a relevant variable since most of the people who left the company do so in their first 10 months in the company.
